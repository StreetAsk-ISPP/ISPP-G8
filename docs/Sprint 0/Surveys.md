**SURVEY 1 : SURVEY ABOUT STREETASK**

link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdOuq7pvP6j8y6-l84DwCX8LFTG1ia9G2a5lMYF6C64PSMiXw/viewform?usp=publish-editor

 **Block 1: Profile and Problem Validation**

- **What is your age range?**

    Objective: Market segmentation. It identifies whether the target audience is young or adult, and which features different sectors prefer.

- **Frequency of outings or travel around the city per week:**

    Objective: To be able to section the questions based on the respondent's number of outings.

- **Have you ever wished you knew real-time information about a location?**

    Objective: Market need validation. It confirms if the problem StreetAsk aims to solve is a real pain point for potential users.

- **In what situations do you think you would use StreetAsk?**

    Objective: Defining User Archetypes and identifying specific use cases (events, crowds, safety, etc.).


**Block 2: Interest and Potential Use**

- **Do you think you would use this application regularly?**

    Objective: Measuring adoption intent. This helps project long-term user retention.

- **What role do you think you would assume most frequently in the application?**

    Objective: To understand how people think they are going to use the app.


**block 3: Trust and System Parameters**

- **How long should a question remain visible?**

    Objective: Establishing the expiration algorithm. It helps define when information is no longer useful and should be cleared from the map to keep it clean.

- **Would you trust answers from strangers if you know they are physically at the location?**

    Objective: Measuring the local trust factor. It determines if you need to add more verification layers.

- **Do you find it necessary to evaluate responses (+/-)?**

    Objective: Quality control system design. It validates if the community is willing to act as content moderators (data curation).


**Block 4: Privacy and notifications**

- **Would you mind sharing your geographical location?**

    Objective: Assessing privacy barriers. StreetAsk is fully GPS-dependent; this question measures user resistance and whether you need to be more transparent about the uses of it.

- **Would it bother you to receive notifications for active questions near you?**

    Objective: Adjusting the Push Notification strategy. Too many notifications lead to uninstalls; too few result in questions going unanswered.


**Block 5: Monetization and incentives**

- **What monetization models would you prefer?**

    Objective: Finding the balance between profitability and user experience. It avoids implementing models that might alienate the community.

- **Level of annoyance per ad type:**

    Objective: Identifying the least intrusive advertising format. This is vital for UI design and ensuring that ads do not break the app's utility.

- **Would you be more likely to respond if you were rewarded?**

    Objective: Validating the engagement engine. It confirms if altruism is sufficient or if a "tokens/points" system is mandatory for the app to function.

- **What upgrades would you like to acquire with the points received?**

    Objective: Defining the app's internal economy. It helps choose between digital rewards (badges, levels) or physical ones (real discounts).


**Block 6: Open feedback and risks**

- **Features you would like / would not like to see:**

    Objective: Creating the Product Roadmap. It prioritizes the development of desired features and avoids wasting resources on those that generate rejection.

- **What could make you NOT use this application?**

    Objective: Risk management question to avoid critical design or conceptual errors.

**SURVEY 2:User Satisfaction Survey**

Link: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfck_onK9gVhseH8WTXH51r9xlgp323Lfm5sp78_au9KnsD4g/viewform?usp=publish-editor

**Block 1: Usability and Performance**

- **How easy was it for you to post your first question or answer?**

    Objective: To measure the learning curve and UI intuitiveness for new users.

- **On a scale of 1 to 5, how would you rate the speed with which you received an answer?**

    Objective: To evaluate the efficiency of the geofencing system and the notification algorithm.

- **Have you encountered any technical errors or 'bugs' while using the application?**

    Objective: To identify performance issues that could negatively impact user retention.


**Block 2: Quality and Safety**

- **Do you consider the answers received to be truthful and useful?**

    Objective: To validate the reliability of user-generated content and the effectiveness of the reputation system.

- **Have you felt safe interacting with other users on the platform?**

    Objective: To assess the social environment and the need for stricter moderation or safety features.


**Block 3: Value Proposition and Loyalty**

- **Has StreetAsk resolved a doubt that you could not have easily resolved with Google Maps or another search?**

    Objective: To confirm the app's Unique Value Proposition (UVP) against major competitors.

- **In what situations would you use StreetAsk?**

    Objective: To identify which real-world scenarios provide the most value to the user.

- **How likely is it that you would recommend StreetAsk to a friend?**

    Objective: To calculate the Net Promoter Score (NPS) and measure organic growth potential.


**Block 4: Reward System**

- **Are you satisfied with the value of the rewards obtained for your answers?**

    Objective: To balance the app's internal economy, ensuring rewards are worth the users' effort.

**Motivation to keep collaborating:**

    Objective: To measure long-term engagement and the "stickiness" of the gamification model.


**Block 5: Open Feedback**

- **Which function do you use the most and which one, in your opinion, should be eliminated?**

    Objective: To prioritize the product roadmap by focusing on core features and removing "bloatware" or unused tools.
