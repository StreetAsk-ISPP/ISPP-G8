# 1. Executive Summary of Monetization

StreetAsk uses a hybrid model (Freemium + B2B Subscription) designed under a Lean structure. It is based on the premise that real-time information has economic value tied to immediacy.

- **Free users:** Act as "data sensors" and financial assets, covering infrastructure costs through mandatory interstitial ads on each post.
- **Premium users:** Generate recurring revenue through a highly optimized micro-payment that removes the psychological barrier of 3 EUR.
- **Business users:** Provide recurring revenue through a flexible monthly subscription aligned with ROI and without permanence.
