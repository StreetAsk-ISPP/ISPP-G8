# AI Usage Report

Throughout this project, our team used Generative AI tools (mainly ChatGPT, Gemini and Copilot) as supportive assistants to speed up certain tasks and improve our workflow. These tools were used as complementary resources to help us understand concepts faster, structure documentation more clearly, and solve minor implementation doubts while developing the MVP.

### 1. Supporting Technical Exploration

During the development of the MVP, AI tools were occasionally used to clarify technical questions and explore possible solutions. Instead of replacing the development process, they served as a quick reference when the team needed guidance or wanted to compare alternative approaches.

Some examples include:

* **Clarifying framework behaviour:** When working with technologies such as Spring Boot, React Native, and Expo, we occasionally asked AI to explain how certain components or configurations work.
* **Troubleshooting small issues:** In some cases, AI helped us understand error messages or suggest potential debugging directions, which allowed us to resolve problems more efficiently.
* **Exploring implementation options:** AI was sometimes used as a brainstorming tool to discuss possible ways to implement specific features before the team decided on the final solution.

All final implementation decisions and code integration were carried out by the team.

### 2. Refining Communication & Documentation

AI was also helpful for improving the quality and clarity of our documentation. It acted mainly as an editing and structuring assistant.

Specifically, we used it for:

* **Grammar and style improvements** in reports written in English.
* **Rephrasing and polishing explanations** to make technical descriptions clearer.
* **Structuring information**, for example converting paragraphs into tables or more readable sections.

This helped us maintain consistent and professional documentation across deliverables.

### 3. Improving Team Organization

To streamline team coordination, AI tools were occasionally used to summarize meeting notes or organize ideas discussed during team conversations. This allowed us to keep track of decisions and action points without spending excessive time on administrative work.

### 4. Creative and Presentation Support

Finally, generative AI was used for minor creative tasks, such as helping design visual elements for presentations or brainstorming ideas for slides and visual assets.
